Select Weapons

Spaceships in Hexode will have a large choice of weaponry. Each weapon has its own arc of fire, which appears in red on the map once at least one weapon is selected. Number on red tiles represent damage dealt to ennemies. Arc of fires from different weapons combines their strength and the numbers simply add up!

If an ennemy ship is caught within a red hex, the corresponding number translates into damage. (The damage system is still a work in progress)
